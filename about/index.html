About bq-mtk

2021-04-05 :: Jose

What is bq-mtk?

bq-mtk is a group of Android Developers, that works on bq Aquaris MTK devices for fun.
We aim to provide the newest Android version to mostly bq Aquaris MTK devices.

Which devices are supported?

Right now, we only support: Aquaris E4.5 (krillin), Aquaris E5 (vegetahd), Aquaris M8 (karin, aquaris_m8), Aquaris M10FHD (freezerfhd), Aquaris M10HD (freezerhd), Aquaris M10 4G (freezerlte)

Where can I get your work?

Our work can be found at Projects page.
We also have our work at some forums like htcmania or xda.

Is the source code available?

Obviously! Android is OOS (Open-Source Software). All code used to build our ROMs, including patches are available at bq-mtk
